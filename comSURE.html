Overview

The COMSUREproductseries is designed to enable seamless connectivity between your machines and the cloud.By leveraging the full potential of Industry 4.0, the COMSURE series products offer a range of benefits to your production facilities. One of the key features of the COMSURE series is its ability to read data from Modbuscompatible devices like meters, VFDs, inverters, and PLCs and transmit it to remote or local servers for real-time data monitoring. The availability of standard communication protocols like MODBUS TCP/IP, MODBUS RTU,SNMP,and MQTT makes it aperfect choice for industrial automation Data acquisition applications.
