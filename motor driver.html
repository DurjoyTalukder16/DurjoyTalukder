Designing Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) for driving the induction motor of a Vane-type fuel pump

Category: PCB

Client: Railway

Project date: 01 February, 2024

Overview

Pumps deliver fuel from a locomotive storage tank to a locomotive engine.

  • Control Mechanism: Linear (Volts/Hz)
  • Input Voltage: 56 to 100 Volt DC (locomotive application)
  • Output Voltage: 0 to 48 volts (line to line)
  • Output Frequency: 0.1 to 60 Hz
  • All parameters are programmable by Windows software through USB.

Key Features

  • Programmable Over and Under Voltage Protection
  • Programmable Maximum RPM
  • Stable Output Voltage with varying input voltage
